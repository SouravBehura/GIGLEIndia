ABOUT US

Awesome Since 2014


Indian Grocery Industries Limited has prevailed as the radical catalyst to serve and evolve the Indian retailers' community. The rate of increase of Unorganised retailers is soaring high and has reached a stressful amount of 1.1 crores. They withstand heavy baggage of not being acknowledged in the market and suffer tremendously to manage revenue and finance. Still, under IGIL, any unorganized retailer can enrol his business to gain links and have a full area of exposure to the market. IGIL works by the vision and cooperation of all retailers, suppliers, manufacturers, consumers, and investors.

There is no exaggeration in the fact that IGIL is a one-way solution of all the difficulties that revolve around retailing. We sign an agreement with underprivileged retailers and provide them with numerous facilities like advertising their store, giving transportation facilities for their products.

We also modernize their stores through technology and provide a broader and greater market exposure. We even stand with them in the financial crisis and help them with all we can provide. We help these stores by managing them in a centralized way. The centralized management enables all information of the stores to reach one particular destination where all decisions are taken regarding the products and services.

The unorganised retailers who assign them with the godown market brand are given advertisement and more exposure. This makes their business flourish more. These retailers get security under this brand name.

By doing so and sailing the unorganized retailers to the shore of being organized, we play our part to level up the GDP. IGIL's vision is to continue the flux in the local community and to take it up to greater heights. We also intend to make every individual flourish in this business.

The IGIL's goal is high and so is our spirit. We have a Mission set for us and we all keen to reach the finish line along with every individual involved with us. Our mission is to build a reliable brand for the customers and to reach them with the best products and services. We also want to be the country's own retailing network with a regulating framework. We wish to help the unorganized retailers in the battle they fight in the market and to make things easy for them by providing them centralized management, infrastructure, technology and training. Through this, they will turn into flourishing, organized retailers. We want to set a global example with our retailing strategy which will be recognized internationally. By completing this mission, we want to contribute our bit in leveling up the GDP and the growing economy of the country.

The IGIL community is all set to work harder than the expectations and set our foot off the gas to focus on our VISION. We would want to emerge into the most promising and trustworthy community - based company in India by 2025. We also intend to get more than 20 lakh suppliers and 50,000 brands along with 1 crore retailers to join us in this ecosystem and succeed.

Our prime vision will be to provide the best products and services to the 100 crore customers and leave no room for disappointment for them. Our vision also includes to gain 70% of the consumers of our population and make them satisfied with our products and services.
